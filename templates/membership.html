Frequently Asked Questions

Is membership free?

Yes! Membership is completely free. We believe that all parents should have access to support and resources.

What are the membership requirements?

There are no specific requirements. If you're a parent, caregiver, or guardian, you're welcome to join.

How do I access member benefits?

Once you register, you'll receive a welcome email with information about accessing programs and resources. You can also check our Programs page for upcoming events.

Can I cancel my membership?

You can opt out of communications at any time, though we hope you'll stay connected with our community.
