How to Use Google Notes for Educational Content

Google Notes (Google Docs/Keep) can help you create and organize educational content before adding it to the system.

Step 1: Create your content in Google Docs or Google Keep
Step 2: Share the document with "Anyone with the link can view" (or keep it private if preferred)
Step 3: Copy the shareable link from Google Docs/Keep
Step 4: When creating a Document or Course in the admin, paste the Google Notes URL in the "Google Notes URL" field
Step 5: You can reference the Google Notes while writing your content, or copy content from it

Benefits:

  • ‚úÖ Draft and edit content collaboratively before publishing
  • ‚úÖ Keep a backup of your content in Google Drive
  • ‚úÖ Easily reference source materials while creating content
  • ‚úÖ Share drafts with team members for review
  • ‚úÖ Access content from anywhere with Google account

Use Alison Courses to Power Certifications

Alison offers free online courses and certifications that you can link directly to P.E.P. certifications.

Step 1: Browse or create a course on Alison.
Step 2: Copy the Alison course URL and course ID/reference.
Step 3: In the admin, open a Certification and paste the link into the Alison course URL field and the ID into Alison course ID.
Step 4: Use the Alison course as the learning path behind that certification.

Benefits:

  • ‚úÖ Access ready-made, high-quality online courses.
  • ‚úÖ Align P.E.P. certifications with recognized online learning.
  • ‚úÖ Reduce time needed to build courses from scratch.
  • ‚úÖ Give members flexible, self-paced learning options.
