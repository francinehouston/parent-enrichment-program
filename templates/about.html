About Us

Program Overview

The Parent Enrichment Program (P.E.P.) is a community-centered initiative operated by MannMade Fitness Productions LLC to strengthen families, support youth athletes, and connect parents with the skills, resources, and relationships they need to thrive.

P.E.P. works at the intersection of education, sports, and community outreach. Parents and guardians are equipped with practical training, certifications, and support systems that empower them to lead in the home, on the sidelines, and in the community.

What We Do

We offer a comprehensive range of programs and resources designed to support parents at every stage of their journey:

  • Educational Workshops: Expert-led sessions on child development, positive parenting, and family wellness
  • Support Groups: Safe spaces for parents to share experiences and learn from one another
  • Resource Library: Access to books, guides, and online resources
  • Community Events: Family-friendly activities that bring our community together
  • One-on-One Support: Personalized guidance for parents facing specific challenges

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've helped thousands of parents build stronger relationships with their children, develop new skills, and connect with a supportive community. Our programs have been shown to:

Core Pillars of the Program

1

Parent Education & Certification

  • Structured courses such as P.C.O.R. (Parent, Coach, Official & Referee or similar leadership-focused training)
  • CPR and basic safety training for parents, guardians, and volunteers
  • Ongoing workshops on communication, mental health awareness, conflict resolution, and youth advocacy
2

Youth Sports & Academic Support

  • Encouraging enrollment of student athletes in organized sports curriculums
  • Reinforcing school attendance, discipline, and academic accountability
  • Connecting families to high-quality coaching, safe training environments, and college or career exposure opportunities
3

Mental Health, Safety, and Wellness

  • Promoting emotional safety, positive team cultures, and supportive peer environments
  • Encouraging families to recognize warning signs and connect with appropriate resources when needed
  • Normalizing conversations around stress, pressure, and life balance for youth and parents
4

Community Partnerships & Incentives

  • Working with local businesses, gyms, training centers, churches, and civic organizations
  • Offering discounts, event access, and reward opportunities for active P.E.P. members
  • Creating pathways for sponsors and donors to support scholarships, camps, and special events

How P.E.P. Works

üë•

a) Membership

  • Parents/guardians enroll in the Parent Enrichment Program as members
  • Each membership is linked to at least one student athlete enrolled in a sports program or curriculum
  • Members commit to program rules, attendance standards, and course requirements
üìú

b) Course & Certification Pathway

Members complete mandatory core courses:

  • P.C.O.R. Course (or equivalent leadership and responsibility training)
  • CPR Certification (or approved CPR/First Aid course)
  • Additional courses designated as mandatory for the season or year
  • Members may pursue additional elective courses for advanced skills or specialty areas
üéâ

c) Events & Engagement

Members attend a minimum number of community events each year, such as:

  • Parent and athlete nights
  • Gala nights with community leaders, scouts, or business partners
  • Workshops, training sessions, and recognition events
  • Events are designed to connect families to opportunities, information, and meaningful relationships
üèÜ

d) Recognition & Real-World Benefits

  • Completed courses and certifications may be recognized through certificates that can be documented and tracked inside the P.E.P. system
  • Over time, the program may align certificates and achievements with real-world opportunities, discounts, or asset-backed rewards managed by the Finance arm of the program (as permitted by law)

Member Commitment Statement

By joining the Parent Enrichment Program, each member agrees to:

  • Act in the best interest of their student athlete and the larger community
  • Follow P.E.P. rules, policies, and attendance standards
  • Treat all participants, officials, coaches, and staff with respect
  • Pursue growth, take required courses, and actively support their student athlete's educational and athletic journey
  • Represent the program with integrity at all times on and off the field

For detailed rules, attendance expectations, and membership bylaws, please refer to the "Parent Enrichment Program Membership Policies, Rules, and Bylaws" document.
