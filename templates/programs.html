Basic Eligibility

To be eligible for membership, a Parent/Guardian Member must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Be a parent, legal guardian, relative, or primary caregiver of a student athlete, or be actively involved in the care and development of youth in the community
  • Agree to abide by program policies, rules, and bylaws

Enrollment Process

Enrollment may include:

  • Attending an orientation session (in-person or virtual) when offered
  • Acknowledging receipt of these policies and bylaws
